Jahseh Onfroy (XXXTentacion)

A Visionary of Emo-Rap and a Voice for the Distressed

The Short, Explosive Career of XXXTentacion

  • 1998: Born in Plantation, Florida.
  • 2013: Begins releasing music on SoundCloud, establishing a raw, emotional style that blended trap, R&B, rock, and punk.
  • 2017: Releases the breakout single "Look at Me!" and his debut album, *17*, which explored themes of depression and introspection, quickly charting worldwide.
  • 2018 (March): Releases his second studio album, *? (Question Mark)*, which reached number one on the Billboard 200 and contained the hit single "Sad!".
  • 2018 (June): Tragically killed at the age of 20 in a robbery attempt in Deerfield Beach, Florida.
  • Legacy: Despite his controversial life and legal issues, his music connected deeply with a large fanbase, defining the emo-rap genre and influencing a generation of artists.

Jahseh Onfroy's musical legacy is marked by his boundary-pushing creativity and his ability to communicate raw, unfiltered emotional pain to millions of listeners.
